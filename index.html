About

I am a robotics engineer in training, currently completing my MSc at Politecnico di Milano, with a focus on Human–Robot Interaction (HRI) and assistive robotics. I enjoy working across the full stack: from mechanical design and modeling to control, teleoperation and user-facing interfaces.

My projects span sign language teaching robots, industrial manipulators, bio-inspired swimming robots, collaborative game-playing robots and VR-based teleoperation. I aim to create systems that are not only technically robust, but also intuitive and accessible for the people who use them.
